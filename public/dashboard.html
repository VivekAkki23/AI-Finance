What is IB Modeler?

IB Modeler is a financial analysis and valuation toolkit designed to help analysts, students, and professionals work like an Investment Banker. It simplifies complex financial modeling by providing tools for:

  • Business Valuation
  • Equity & Market Analysis
  • Comparable Company Analysis (Comps)
  • M&A and Deal Modeling

Whether you're preparing pitch decks, evaluating investments, or studying finance — IB Modeler helps you make data-driven decisions with clarity.

Key Concepts in Finance

  • Valuation: Determining what a company is worth using DCF, Net Income, Assets, etc.
  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF): Predicting future cash and calculating present value.
  • Multiples & Comps: Comparing similar companies using ratios like P/E, EV/EBITDA.
  • Market Sentiment: The general mood of investors (Bullish vs Bearish).
